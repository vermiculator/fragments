The sharing of knowledge and ideas is greatly benefitted by citation of sources, inspirations and related concepts. This applies not only on the internet or in academic works but also in ordinary discussion - give due credit and [leave breadcrumbs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Breadcrumbs#In_popular_culture) for fellow and future thinkers.

## Mentions
- "second-hand images, as the author argued in the text, “misguide our imagination and lead us into Error by obtruding upon us the Imaginations of a Person, possibly, more ignorant than our selves” (original capitalization, Hooke and Derham, 1726, p.294)."
