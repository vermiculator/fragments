- beyond [[playgrounds]], playspaces can be any context of play
- nurseries and schools
- home spaces, playrooms
- youth clubs
- casinos
- interactive exhibitions, museum spaces etc
- playing fields, tennis courts and other large recreational flat areas
- wooded and rural spaces for large games, camp games, LARPs and paintball
- interior LARPs, the space between TTRPGs and LARPs
- strictly-tabletop
- theatre of the mind
- wider space of board/puzzle/card/kids games in open area eg Jenga
- temporary spaces of carnival and event games eg giant Jenga
- theme parks, immersive spaces, carnival houses on wheels, rides, line entertainments
- play overtaking non-play space temporarily
- virtual spaces, [[thoughts/video games]]
- physical and digital realities
