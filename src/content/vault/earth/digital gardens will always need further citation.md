title: digital gardens will always need further citation
---

As a collection of [[living documents]], a [[digital gardens|digital garden]] is continuously updated, with notes of varying completeness. This may include new ideas that haven't yet been backed up by reputable sources. As [[digital gardens are never 'finished']], there will always be areas that need further citation.
