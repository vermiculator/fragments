and, increasingly, you kinda CAN Google your memories. This is not desirable for numerous [[data privacy]] reasons already without engaging in any more specific niches of [[philosophy]] or [[techno-politics]]

the topic of 'search' is already complex, the topic of memory recall is already complex, lets not hand over this power to a single tech conglomerate for a start, and then we can discuss the issues of associative memory, indexing, second brains and lossy data capture
