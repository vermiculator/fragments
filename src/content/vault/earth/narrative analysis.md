title: narrative analysis

- not fragmented selves
- interviews common for narrative data
- life-history data or broad across domains eg family and work
- episodic focus on a specific life experience
- interview protocols eg McAdams 1993
- two broad phases, descriptive and interpretative, 
- descriptive - summaries and narrative structure, linkages, narrative strategies
- interpretative - how identity is constructed through the narrative, what is included/omitted/repeated, socio-political context, sense-making, etc
