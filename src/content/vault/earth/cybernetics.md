- science of systems

- assuming [[neutrality and objectivity|no objective truth]]
- applied epistemology

## history

- a field developed for war, again ([[tech for war]])
- term coined by mathematician Norbert Weiner in WW1
- 'predictor device' to increase aiming accuracy of anti-aircraft guns
- early principles of 'control' and 'communication'
- early focus on self-regulating systems, adaptation in response to environment
- 60s second gen linked in ecological and philosophical contexts
- later principles of 'observation' and 'influence'
- 'creative acts with minimal human intervention' - [[computers with agency]]
