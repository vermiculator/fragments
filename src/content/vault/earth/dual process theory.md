A framework for understanding thought processes: we are in one of two states, where the first is autonomous action and the second is deliberative thought. 

- referred to as 'System 1 and System 2'
- traces its roots back to William James (American philosopher and psychologist)
- conceptualises System 2 as evolutionarily newer, the start of conscious thought
- theories brought to the mainstream by Danny Kahneman’s “_Thinking, Fast and Slow_”
