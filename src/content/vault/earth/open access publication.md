- push for access to prior knowledges, prior research, current scientific consensus
- open access isn't always equitable and often requires authors to pay for it

---

- Green Open Access is usually free and has licensing dictated by publisher
- Gold Open Access incurs an Article Processing Charge (APC) - extortinate prices, published on an open licence (usually CC BY), but some venues only use this kind
