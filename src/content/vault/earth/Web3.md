The [[tech bros]]' vision of a new Web - some interesting innovations but not nearly as widely-relevant or socially impactful as the potential of [[Web 3.0]]. The two are often conflated due to crossover in designation and terminology (eg 'federation' and 'distributed' and 'peer to peer') but they actually form distinct movements with differing priorities.
