- in this context, generally referring to the defunding, dismantling and abolition of police, prisons, and the justice system as it currently stands
- does not mean lack of regulation, rule or behavioural reform, but removal of right for the state to violently enforce laws, prioritises in-community handling of situations more safely and respectfully of all parties involved, perusing real justice or solace for all parties involved, mediation and holistic care
