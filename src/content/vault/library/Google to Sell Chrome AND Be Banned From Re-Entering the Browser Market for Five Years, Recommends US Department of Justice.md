title: "Google to Sell Chrome AND Be Banned From Re-Entering the Browser Market for Five Years, Recommends US Department of Justice"
summary: The US Department of Justice has filed a proposal to force Google to sell its Chrome browser and ban the company from re-entering the browser market for five years. The plan also includes restrictions on Google's acquisitions and payments that maintain its search engine dominance. Google's response has been critical, labeling the proposals as harmful to consumers and technology innovation.
