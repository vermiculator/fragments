summary: The text discusses the concept of "vibrant matter," highlighting how materials like food, electricity, and waste actively influence human life and society. It argues against viewing matter as passive, proposing instead that it has intrinsic vitality that interacts with humans in meaningful ways. The author seeks to reframe our understanding of self and nature, emphasizing the interconnectedness of human and nonhuman elements.
