summary: The text discusses the challenges and experiences of squats and
  autonomous spaces in Europe, particularly in Athens and other cities facing
  evictions. It emphasizes that squatting is a response to housing crises and
  offers alternative ways of living and organizing communities. The author
  reflects on past projects, the importance of collective action, and the
  lessons learned from evictions.
