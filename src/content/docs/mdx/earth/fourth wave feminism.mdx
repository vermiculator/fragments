* some consider a shift from around 2010
* widely influenced by social media platforms
* and also beginning to be conducted more on/through these platforms
* focuses on sexual harassment, body shaming, etc
* empowerment through community building
* countering thoughts of 'post-feminism'
* somewhat hand-in-hand to wider collective liberatory movements
* but also strengthened the [TERF](/mdx/earth/terfs) movement (trans-exclusionary radical feminists)
