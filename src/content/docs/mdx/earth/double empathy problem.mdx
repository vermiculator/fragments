non-autistic people often view autistic people as lacking empathy. on further examination, both parties are often having trouble communicating with the other in a way that makes them feel understood and cared for, as they are communicating via different methods. in understanding the thought processes or those with different neurotypes to us we can better communicate in a way that respects both parties.
