* for me and therefore for you
  * eg look after yourself so you can care for others
* for you and therefore for you
  * eg treating someone in a tit-for-tat
  * eg [compersion](/mdx/compersion)
* for me and therefore for us
  * eg collecting resources, giving overflow
* for us and therefore for me
  * eg collecting abundant resources, taking overflow
* for us, just for us
  * I am inherently one of us
* for you, for you
  * I cannot determine if you'll interpret this as a gift or loan or debt, etc
